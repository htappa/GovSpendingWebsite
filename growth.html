Fastest Growing Countries: Healthcare and Education Expenditure

Annual growth is an important measurement for a country's economy, and this section focuses on the growth of healthcare and education expenditure for each of the eleven countries over a period of nine years for healthcare expenditure and five years for education expenditure. The tables show each country's annual growth in terms of fixed value and percentage, and the interactive maps show percentage growth, with lighter shades of blue representing lower growth and darker shades of blue representing higher growth.
